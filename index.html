About Me

After graduating from Skidmore College I headed West to Los Angeles where I spent seven oddly exciting/excitingly odd years acting in commercials and working as Music and Marketing Director at Kingdom 2 Music. Seeking to round out my skillset, I then moved to St. Petersburg, FL to learn how to code at The Iron Yard.

It quickly became clear that this career-shift didn't mean I had to turn my back on the Creative Process. I love the act of taking a blank white page and a series of ideas and turning that into a fully-functioning website, game, or app. I've recently accepted several freelance jobs and have discovered that my background in marketing, copywriting, and customer service create a smooth and easy dynamic between my clients and I. I look forward to continuing to direct that passion toward countless projects to come- perhaps even yours.
